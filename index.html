Frequently Asked Questions

How does the UC top-up work?
Enter your BGMI User ID on this page, choose a UC pack, and finish the payment. The UC will be added directly to your in-game account. No redemption code required.
How long does delivery take?
Most top-ups are delivered within a few minutes of successful payment. In rare cases, it may take up to 24 hours. If you don't see the UC in your app, please restart the app and check again.
What should I do if I entered the wrong User ID?
UC credited to another account cannot be reversed or transferred. Please double-check your BGMI User ID before confirming the purchase.
Can I gift UC to someone else?
Absolutely! You can buy UC for any BGMI player. If you're purchasing for the first time, simply enter your friend's BGMI User ID and their UC will be topped up directly. If you've already saved your own BGMI ID from a previous purchase, tap "Change" on the BGMI ID section, enter your friend's ID, and proceed with the purchase. The UC will be delivered to whichever ID is shown at checkout.
What payment methods are accepted?
UCify accepts UPI as well as debit and credit cards. The available payment methods will be displayed at checkout.
Payment done but UC not received, what should I do?
Wait about 30 minutes, then check whether the UC has been credited to your BGMI account. If it still hasn't arrived, contact UCify support with your order ID and BGMI User ID.
Does UC purchased from UCify count in the BGMI UC UP EVENT?
Yes, absolutely! Any UC purchased through UCify is officially transferred to your account and fully counts towards all in-game events, including the BGMI UC UP Event.
